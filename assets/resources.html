Different Fox Rescues

Down below are different fox rescues. These rescues go out of their way to ensure the well-being of all the foxes they rescue.

  • For Fox Sake Wildlife Rescue

    * For Fox Sake Wildlife Rescue is a nonprofit wildlife rehabilitation facility. They are permitted by Tennessee Wildlife Resources Agency and professionally certified through the International Wildlife Rehabilitation Council. They work tirelessly to rescue orphaned, injured, or sick wild animals. They promote peaceful coexistence to protect animals and humans from unnecessary conflict. The animals rescued return to the wild after 90-120 days of rehabilitation in human care.

  • Fox Friends Sanctuary INC

    * They are a Florida licensed nonprofit exotic animal sanctuary that specializes in foxes of all species. Their top priority is the health and happiness of their foxes.

  • Fox Tales Florida Rescue & Sanctuary

    * Fox Tales Florida Rescue & Sanctuary rescues captive bred foxes. All past owners are allowed to come and visit their surrendered foxes. Their goal is to give the foxes a good life with enrichment, proper diet, and all the love required for the best possible life for the rest of their years.

  • Save A Fox

    * Save A Fox rescues a lot of foxes from fur farms. They are the largest red fox rescue in the United States. They rescue captive foxes like foxes from fur farms or foxes that are surrendered from people who got them from breeders.
